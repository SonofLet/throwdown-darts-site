Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Overview

Throwdown Darts is designed with privacy in mind. The app stores all data locally on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. Throwdown Darts does not require an account, does not access your contacts, location, camera, or any other device sensors, and does not connect to the internet.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app saves game history and statistics locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

Throwdown Darts does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Throwdown Darts does not collect data from any users, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at support@throwdowndarts.app or visit our support page.
