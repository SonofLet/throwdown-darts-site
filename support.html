Frequently Asked Questions

How do I score using the dartboard?

Tap directly on the dartboard where your dart landed. The ring zones are widened to make doubles and trebles easy to hit with your finger. You can switch to the number keypad using the # icon.

What happens when I bust in X01?

If your score would go below zero or land on 1, it's a bust. Your score resets to what it was at the start of your turn and play passes to the next player.

How does Cricket scoring work?

Hit numbers 15–20 and Bull three times each to close them. Once you've closed a number, hitting it again scores points — unless your opponent has also closed it. First to close all 7 numbers with equal or more points wins.

Where is my game history stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. If you delete the app, your history will be lost.

Can I play with more than 2 players?

Yes — up to 4 players on one device. Add players on the New Game screen before starting.
